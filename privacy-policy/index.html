Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026.

RFT Recruitment collects and uses personal information so we can respond to hiring enquiries, candidate registrations and CV submissions.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, company, role requirements, career preferences, CV, work history and messages you send through this website.

How We Use It

We use this information to respond to enquiries, discuss suitable roles or candidates, manage recruitment activity, keep appropriate records and improve our service.

CVs And Candidate Data

We will not send your CV or personal details to an employer without your knowledge. Candidate information is used only for recruitment-related conversations and suitable opportunities.

Storage And Security

Website enquiries and CV submissions are stored using trusted service providers, including Supabase. We take reasonable steps to keep personal information secure and restrict access to authorised users.

Your Rights

You can ask us to update, correct or delete your information. You can also ask us to stop contacting you about recruitment opportunities.

Contact

For privacy requests, email hello@rftrecruitment.co.uk.
