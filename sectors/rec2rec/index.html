Roles We Place

Recruitment Roles We Place

From resourcers and junior consultants to directors and in-house talent leads, we place across the full recruitment function.

01

Resourcer

Candidate sourcing, CV screening and interview coordination within a recruitment team. A common entry point for people joining the industry, or an experienced resourcer making a lateral move to a better environment.

02

Recruitment Consultant

Full 360 delivery across business development and candidate management, or specialist 180-degree delivery within a defined desk. Billing history and desk type are always part of the conversation.

03

Senior Recruitment Consultant

Established billers who manage complex client relationships, mentor junior consultants and operate with a high degree of autonomy. Billing threshold expectations vary significantly by agency and desk type.

04

Principal Consultant

Typically a high-biller with a leadership or mentoring component. Often the stepping stone between senior consultant and management for those who want to develop without a full people management role.

05

Managing Consultant

Team management responsibility combined with a personal billing target. The most common first management role in a recruitment business - and widely acknowledged as the hardest transition to get right.

06

Recruitment Manager

Line management of a team with accountability for revenue, headcount and consultant development. Often focused on one specific desk or vertical within the agency structure.

07

Business Development Manager

New client acquisition for a recruitment business. Sometimes combined with account management; always requires credibility and confidence in front of senior hiring managers and procurement leads.

08

Talent Acquisition Partner

In-house recruiter embedded within a single employer, responsible for direct sourcing, process management and stakeholder relationships across defined business areas and hiring manager groups.

09

Head of Talent

Senior in-house leadership across the full talent function, including employer brand, pipeline strategy and team management. A growing role as businesses build more sophisticated internal TA capabilities.

10

Recruitment Director

Board or near-board leadership within an agency or in-house talent function. Accountable for revenue targets, team growth and market positioning at the highest level of the business.

11

Onboarding Specialist

Post-offer candidate management and compliance, particularly in volume or outsourced recruitment environments where the process after offer accepted is as commercially important as the search itself.

12

Account Manager (Recruitment)

Client relationship management within an RPO or MSP model, focused on service delivery, compliance and hiring manager relationships across large enterprise accounts.

For Employers

Why Employers Choose RFT

We understand your world, assess recruiters honestly and operate with complete discretion.

We Understand Your World

Rec2Rec is only credible when the consultant doing the search understands billing structures, desk types and what makes a recruiter perform. RFT's Rec2Rec practice is led by consultants with direct agency experience. We brief candidates properly because we understand what the role involves from the inside.

Honest Assessment of Candidates

Recruiters know how to present well. We go beyond the interview performance and look at billing history, tenure, desk type and reasons for movement. You receive an honest picture of each candidate - not a polished one constructed specifically for the process.

Confidentiality as Standard

Rec2Rec is a small world. We manage every search with full discretion, and we never approach your existing team or share client details without explicit authorisation. Your business, your headcount plans and your pipeline remain confidential throughout the process.

For Candidates

Why Candidates Work With RFT

Genuine options, real market intelligence and experienced guidance on the counter-offer conversation.

Genuine Options, Not Volume

We do not carpet-bomb your details across every agency in Manchester. We identify a small number of opportunities that genuinely fit your experience, billing level and career direction, then brief you properly before any introduction is made. Your reputation is protected throughout.

Advice on the Market

We know which agencies are growing, which are losing consultants and which have the infrastructure to support a strong biller. That intelligence is valuable when you are making a career decision that affects your earnings, your clients and your long-term trajectory in the industry.

Understanding the Counter-Offer Dynamic

We have seen enough recruiters accept counter-offers and return to the market six months later to know how to have that conversation early. We help you work through it properly so you make the right decision for your career, not just the expedient one in the moment.
