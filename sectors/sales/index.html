Roles We Place

Sales Roles We Recruit

From first-hire business development executives to national sales directors, we recruit across the full commercial sales function.

01

Business Development Executive

Outbound prospecting, pipeline building and new business conversion. Typically the first sales hire in a team or an entry point for candidates building a B2B sales career.

02

Account Executive

Managing existing relationships and identifying upsell and cross-sell opportunities within a defined account base. Strong retention metrics matter as much as new business wins.

03

Sales Manager

Frontline team leadership with responsibility for individual quotas, pipeline management and coaching junior members of the team. We look for player-managers who can lead by example.

04

Business Development Manager

Senior-level new business focus, often with autonomy over territory strategy and the ability to work to longer sales cycles at higher contract values.

05

Key Account Manager

Strategic relationship management for a business's most commercially significant clients. Candidates must demonstrate how they have grown and retained accounts over time.

06

Sales Director

Board-level or near-board commercial leadership across a revenue function. Often owns the go-to-market strategy, team structure and partner relationships simultaneously.

07

Field Sales Representative

Territory-based face-to-face selling, common in FMCG, medical devices, manufacturing and logistics. A clean driving licence and a track record of meeting territory targets is standard.

08

Telesales Executive

High-volume inbound or outbound calling, often in financial services, utilities or SaaS environments. Call quality, conversion rate and daily activity are the primary performance measures.

09

Internal Sales Executive

Desk-based sales support and account management, frequently working alongside a field team to qualify leads and close smaller opportunities.

10

National Sales Manager

Multi-territory or multi-region sales leadership. Candidates must show they can manage remote teams, set strategy independently and report at board level.

11

Pre-Sales Consultant

Technical sales support bridging product knowledge and commercial conversations, most common in IT, software and professional services environments.

12

Partnership Manager

Channel and alliance relationship management, typically within tech, media or financial services. Candidates need both sales credibility and the ability to build long-term collaborative relationships.
