Commercial & Procurement Recruitment

Commercial and finance roles demand a level of precision that other disciplines do not. An error in procurement, a gap in compliance knowledge or a misaligned commercial analyst can have measurable financial consequences within weeks. RFT recruits across the full commercial function: purchasing and procurement, commercial finance, contract management, compliance and legal support. We work with manufacturers, professional services firms, infrastructure businesses and public sector organisations across Manchester and the wider North West.

Roles We Place

Commercial Roles We Recruit

From purchasing assistants to commercial directors, we recruit across the full procurement and commercial function.

01

Procurement Manager

Category strategy, supplier selection and contract negotiation across defined spend areas. Candidates with CIPS accreditation and demonstrable savings track records are in consistent demand.

02

Buyer

Day-to-day purchasing across a defined category or supplier base. Experience within a specific commodity or industry vertical (e.g. engineering, FMCG, construction) carries significant weight with clients.

03

Commercial Manager

P&L accountability, contract management and commercial performance across business units or projects. Common in construction, infrastructure and professional services environments.

04

Contracts Manager

Drafting, negotiating and administering commercial contracts. Often works alongside legal counsel and procurement teams to manage risk and performance obligations across the supply chain.

05

Commercial Analyst

Financial modelling, pricing analysis and commercial reporting. Strong Excel, SQL and data visualisation skills are increasingly standard at this level across all sectors.

06

Supply Chain Manager

End-to-end supply chain oversight including demand planning, logistics coordination and supplier relationship management. ERP experience is usually a prerequisite for these roles.

07

Compliance Officer

Regulatory adherence, policy implementation and internal audit support. Sector-specific knowledge (financial services, construction, pharmaceutical) is often the deciding factor in appointment.

08

Category Manager

Strategic ownership of a defined spend category, typically within large enterprise or public sector environments. CIPS qualification and evidence of stakeholder management at senior level expected.

09

Bid Manager / Estimator

Tender preparation, commercial modelling and bid submission coordination. Common in construction, facilities management and IT services businesses bidding on public and private contracts.

10

Legal Secretary / Paralegal

Document management, matter administration and client liaison within legal and professional services environments. Accuracy, discretion and sector familiarity are essential.

11

Purchasing Assistant

Junior purchasing support: raising purchase orders, managing supplier queries and maintaining procurement records. A strong entry-level point for graduates entering the commercial function.

12

Strategic Sourcing Manager

High-level sourcing strategy across global or multi-region supply chains. Candidates typically have extensive negotiation experience and a track record of commercial value delivery at enterprise level.
