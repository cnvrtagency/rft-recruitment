Office Support & Administration Recruitment

Office support and administration roles are the backbone of every business, and getting them right is more commercially important than many organisations realise. A poor hire at PA, Office Manager or Executive Assistant level creates friction across entire teams. RFT recruits across the full administrative function, from reception and coordination roles through to senior EAs supporting C-suite executives. We work with employers across Manchester and the wider North West, placing candidates who are organised, proactive and genuinely professional in how they operate.

About This Sector

Administration Recruitment That Goes Deeper

The office support market moves quickly. Good PAs and EAs are rarely available for long, and the candidates who can manage complex diaries, coordinate international travel and handle confidential matters with discretion are in consistent demand. RFT maintains an active pool of office support professionals at every level, which means we can respond with pace rather than relying on a slow job board cycle.

We take the time to understand working culture and management style, because fit matters as much as skills in a support function that sits close to senior leadership. An EA who is technically competent but cannot read a principal's priorities creates problems. We assess both - and we tell you what we find at the briefing stage rather than leaving it to interview day to discover.

Roles We Place

Office Support Roles We Recruit

From reception through to C-suite EA, we recruit across the full administrative function.

01

Receptionist

First-point-of-contact roles managing switchboards, visitor management and general office coordination. Presentation, communication and reliability are the defining qualities we look for.

02

Administrator

General administrative support across departments: data entry, document management, scheduling and inbox handling. Attention to detail and software proficiency are consistently expected.

03

Office Manager

End-to-end office operations including facilities, supplier management, HR administration and team coordination. Experience managing a physical office environment is usually a prerequisite.

04

Executive Assistant

Complex diary management, board-level correspondence, travel logistics and confidential document handling. We place EAs at MD, Director and C-suite level who operate as true business partners.

05

Personal Assistant

Dedicated one-to-one support for senior individuals. Candidates must demonstrate discretion, initiative and the ability to manage upward when necessary.

06

Team Secretary

Shared administrative support across professional teams in legal, financial and professional services environments. Accurate document production and formatting skills are essential.

07

Data Entry Clerk

High-volume, accuracy-led roles focused on database updating, records management and CRM input. Speed and precision are both measured at this level.

08

Operations Coordinator

Cross-functional coordination roles that sit between administration and operations management. Candidates typically have strong process skills and experience working across departments.

09

Project Coordinator

Administrative support within project environments, including tracking milestones, managing documentation and liaising with stakeholders. PM software exposure is increasingly expected.

10

Business Support Assistant

Flexible, generalist roles supporting multiple business functions. Often the entry point for candidates building an administrative career across different sectors.

11

Customer Service Administrator

Inbound query handling combined with administrative duties, common in financial services, insurance and professional services environments.

12

HR Administrator

Onboarding administration, payroll liaison, absence tracking and employee records management. Often sits within a shared services or HR team.
