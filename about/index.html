About RFT  ·  Manchester

We built RFT because doing it right matters
more than doing it quickly

Most recruitment agencies are built around volume. Fill the role, meet the deadline, move on. When the placement does not work out, the cycle starts again. Martyna Dudek founded RFT Recruitment because she had seen that model up close and knew there was a better way.

Hire Talent Browse Jobs

Founded by Martyna Dudek

A different approach
from day one.

After years working in recruitment, Martyna grew frustrated with the pressure to place candidates quickly over placing them correctly. Deadlines drove decisions. CVs went to clients before candidates were properly understood. Roles were filled and then refilled because the fit was never right in the first place.

RFT was founded to fix that. The agency is built around a simple principle. A placement should only ever need to happen once. That means taking the time to properly understand every role, every business and every candidate before anything else happens.

Martyna Dudek
Founder & Managing Director, RFT Recruitment

How We Work

Rigorous before we ever pick up the phone.

Before a CV reaches a decision maker's desk, RFT has already done the work.

Send a Brief

Candidates are interviewed in depth, assessed against the role and briefed thoroughly. Employers receive a shortlist of people who have been properly vetted, not a pile of profiles to sift through.

It takes longer to do it this way. That is the point.

"A placement should only ever need to happen once."

What We Stand For

Four principles.
Every placement.

01

Right fit over fast fill

A placement that does not work costs everyone time, money and momentum. RFT prioritises getting the fit right over getting the role filled quickly every time.

02

Extended candidate assessment

Every candidate goes through a proper interview process with RFT before being put forward. No CV reaches a client until we are confident the fit is right.

03

One placement, done properly

The goal is not to fill a role and move on. The goal is to make a placement that sticks. RFT measures success by performance, not by roles closed.

04

Honest communication throughout

If a candidate is not right, RFT will say so. If a brief needs refining, RFT will say that too. Clear, direct communication makes good placements happen.
